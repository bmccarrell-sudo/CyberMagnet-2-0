What Is This?

A Thorough, Honest Assessment of Every Layer of Your Website.

Most websites have problems their owners don't know about — slow load times that kill conversions, technical SEO issues that suppress rankings, and conversion gaps that let leads slip away silently. None of these show up on your analytics dashboard. They require a dedicated audit to surface.

The Website Audit delivers a complete picture of what's working, what's broken, and what to fix first. Every finding is documented with a clear explanation of why it matters, what impact fixing it will have, and exactly what action to take. You leave with a prioritised roadmap — not a list of problems with no solutions.

What's Delivered

Every Component Explained.

Each component covers a critical layer of website performance — together they give you a complete picture of what needs attention and in what order.

⚙️
Technical SEO Analysis
We crawl your site to identify technical issues that prevent Google from properly indexing your pages — broken links, redirect chains, duplicate content, missing sitemaps, and structured data errors.
⚡
Page Speed & Core Web Vitals
We run your site through Google's performance tools and identify the specific assets and code causing slow load times — with precise recommendations for each issue.
📱
Mobile Usability Review
We test your site on multiple mobile viewports, checking touch target sizes, font readability, layout shifts, and the mobile user experience end-to-end.
🔍
On-Page SEO Review
We review your title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, keyword targeting, image alt text, and internal linking across your key pages.
🎯
Conversion Architecture Review
We assess your CTA placement, form friction, trust signal positioning, headline clarity, and user journey logic — identifying where visitors are dropping off and why.
📋
Prioritised Fix Roadmap
Every issue is documented with an effort score, an impact score, and a specific recommended action — sorted so you know exactly what to fix first for the fastest results.

Best Used For

When to Use This Micro-System.

The Website Audit delivers the most value in these specific situations.

🔧
Before a Redesign
Don't spend money rebuilding a site with the same underlying problems. The audit tells you exactly what to fix, preserve, and improve — before a single line of code is written.
📉
Traffic or Rankings Dropped
If your organic traffic has fallen without a clear cause, a technical SEO audit often surfaces the exact issue — a crawl error, a penalty trigger, or a Core Web Vitals failure.
💡
Pre-Agency Briefing
Hand a detailed audit to an SEO agency or developer as your project brief. You arrive with specific findings, clear priorities, and a benchmark to measure improvements against.
