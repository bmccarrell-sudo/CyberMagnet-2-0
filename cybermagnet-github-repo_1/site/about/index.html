The Background

The founder of CyberMagnet brings a decade of hands-on experience across digital marketing's most impactful disciplines — SEO, conversion funnels, brand strategy, paid advertising, and AI-powered automation. Not as a specialist siloed in one lane, but as someone who has led full-stack digital campaigns from first touchpoint to closed deal.

Before founding CyberMagnet, they worked across a range of industries — from local trades and service businesses to SaaS companies and e-commerce brands — developing a rare ability to spot the exact gap between where a business is and where it could be.

Why CyberMagnet

The frustration was always the same: brilliant businesses being underserved by agencies that treated them as small fish in a big pond. Too much process. Too little results. Too many excuses.

CyberMagnet was the answer — an agency with the strategic depth to compete at the highest level, but built around the kind of client relationship where you actually know who's working on your account and why every decision gets made.

The Philosophy

Growth is a system. Good design builds credibility. Smart SEO creates compounding leverage. A well-built funnel turns traffic into revenue. AI automation scales what works. When these elements connect, a business transforms.

That's not theory — it's the blueprint behind every successful client engagement CyberMagnet has run. The goal was never to be the biggest agency. It was to be the most effective one, for the businesses that needed it most.

To be the most trusted digital growth partner for local businesses in Dallas and beyond — the agency that business owners recommend to their peers because the results spoke for themselves, and the partnership felt like it was built for them.
