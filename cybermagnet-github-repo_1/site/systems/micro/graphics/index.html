What Is This?

Graphics That Look Intentional, Not Improvised.

The visual quality of your social media content directly affects how potential customers perceive your business. Low-quality or inconsistent graphics signal an unprofessional operation — even when your actual service is excellent. First impressions are made in milliseconds, and visuals carry more weight than any caption.

The Quick Fix Graphic Bundle delivers professionally designed, brand-consistent graphics ready for immediate use. Every piece is created to your brand guidelines (or we recommend a palette if you don't have one), supplied in all the formats you need, and delivered with editable source files so you can update them yourself going forward without coming back to us for every change.

Problems We Solve

The Pain Points This Addresses.

1

Inconsistent visuals making your brand look unprofessional

Mismatched fonts, colours, and styles across your posts signal that your business isn't detail-oriented. Consistent, professional graphics immediately elevate your perceived quality.

2

No time or skills to design graphics yourself

Most business owners shouldn't be spending their time in Canva. A graphic bundle gives you professional assets without the learning curve — and editable files for when you need small updates later.

3

Graphics that don't fit platform specifications

Using the wrong image dimensions means your graphics get cropped, distorted, or display incorrectly on mobile. Every piece we deliver is sized precisely for the platform it's built for.
