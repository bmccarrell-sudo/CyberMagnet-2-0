FAQs

Questions? Answered.

Is this actually free? What's the catch?+
Completely free — no credit card, no contract, no obligation. We offer audits because it's the best way to show you what we're capable of. Some clients hire us afterwards; many just use the report themselves. Either outcome is fine with us.
How is this different from a free Semrush or Ahrefs scan?+
Automated tools give you data — we give you answers. A CyberMagnet strategist reviews your site manually, cross-references the data with your industry and competitors, and writes actionable recommendations specific to your situation. You get insight, not just a spreadsheet.
What do you do with my website and contact information?+
We use your website URL to run the audit and your email to deliver it. We do not sell data, share it with third parties, or add you to cold outreach lists. You may receive one follow-up email after your audit is delivered — that's it unless you reach out to us.
My site is new — is it too early for an audit?+
Not at all. In fact, catching and fixing technical issues early is the best time to do it. We'll flag any structural problems before they compound, and give you a roadmap for building authority from the ground up the right way.
How long until I see results if I implement the recommendations?+
Technical fixes can show impact within 2–4 weeks once Google re-crawls your site. On-page and content improvements typically compound over 60–90 days. Link authority takes longer — 3 to 6 months for meaningful movement — but the earlier you start, the sooner the compounding begins.
